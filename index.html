The Visionary
Pillars.

01

Philosophy

True social work goes beyond giving a single meal—it is about restoring a person’s right to live with honor. Our foundation operates on a 360-degree rehabilitation model. We identify the most vulnerable individuals on the streets, understand their deepest crisis, and provide them with a complete ecosystem of survival: a roof over their head, a source of income, and a family to lean on.

02

Journey

What began as a heartfelt mission to document and help the invisible souls on the street has now grown into a massive humanitarian movement backed by lakhs of well-wishers. We bridge the gap between social neglect and human dignity, proving that with collective action, we can wipe out homelessness and despair.

Our 3 Mega Projects

Housing the Homeless

Mane Kattsikododu

"Turning Shacks into Safe Spaces"

Temporary shelters cannot withstand the harsh realities of life. We identify families and individuals living on the verges of roads or broken huts and construct concrete, safe, and permanent houses for them. We give them an address, safety, and a foundation for a better future.

Livelihood Empowerment

Shop Setup & Micro-Businesses

"Ending Dependency, Enabling Self-Reliance"

True empowerment means making someone capable of earning their own bread. For individuals who have the will to work but lack resources, we set up small-scale businesses and grocery/tea shops. By funding their setup costs, we ensure they never have to beg on the streets again.

Healthcare & Old Age Home

Ashrama Support

"A Sanctuary of Love for Abandoned Elders"

For elderly individuals suffering from chronic illnesses or abandoned by their families, our Ashram serves as a lifelong home. We provide 24/7 medical supervision, specialized geriatric care, sanitation, and nutritious meals daily, wrapped in unconditional affection.

FAQs

Everything you need to know about our initiatives, if you have any other questions please reach out to us at: voiceofpeoplegvt@gmail.com

Voice Of People is India's most trusted online donation platform. We help you support verified non-profits and causes you care about, ensuring your contributions create a real, measurable impact on the ground.

You can easily donate by selecting a mission or a specific NGO, choosing your desired donation amount, and completing the transaction using our secure payment gateway (Cards, UPI, Net Banking, etc.).

The minimum donation amount typically depends on the payment gateway requirements, but generally, you can start making an impact with as little as ₹100.

Upon successful donation, you will automatically receive an 80G tax exemption receipt on your registered email address, which you can use while filing your IT returns.

We host a wide array of causes ranging from child education, hunger eradication, and elderly care to environmental protection, animal welfare, and women empowerment.

We believe in 100% transparency. You will receive regular email updates, impact reports, and occasionally pictures or messages from the ground showing exactly how your funds are being utilized.

Monthly donations provide a steady and predictable source of funding for NGOs, allowing them to plan long-term interventions and execute ongoing, sustainable projects effectively without the fear of sudden resource depletion.
